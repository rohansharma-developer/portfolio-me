about

The creative revolution is in progress. Don’t get left behind.

Growing up in the small Indian city of Meerut, also known as the sports city, I didn't have much interest in sports, therefore I didn't join school teams or travel to the district level to play these activities. Instead, I always played physical games for pleasure. After the corona virus epidemic began in March, I became bored and spent virtually every day playing video games. At the end of May, my sister suggested that I take some coding courses. Then, after finally starting a CSS course that my sister had given me, I began my coding career. But it turned out that I enjoyed coding and wanted to learn more about it.
‍
So I began taking more web development classes in other languages in order to truly understand how to create real-world websites, after which I learned various things such as how to design a full-fledged web-portal using PHP, among other things. As I finished building a website, I realised that I lacked the skills to create graphics and photographs for it, so I began learning photoshopping, generating mockups, editing films, and animations, among other things. After that, I've settled on creating attractive websites with a modern UI that strike the ideal combination of aesthetics and cleanliness.
